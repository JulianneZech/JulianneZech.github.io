Julianne Zech


I'm a software engineer analyst at BlackRock in NYC working in the Aladdin Product Group.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Georgetown University in 2020 with majors in computer science and math. My coursework included algorithms, AI, statistics, accounting, international finance, economics, negotiation, and public speaking.

I earned a $56K full tuition scholarship and a $5K grant from the Clare Boothe Luce Foundation awarded to 2 Georgetown women STEM leaders by the Provost and faculty. I won the Department of Computer Science Excellence Award, awarded to the top 2% of juniors and seniors who demonstrated the highest levels of leadership and academic achievement.

I'm passionate about technology, product, finance, and sustainability (opportunities for women in technology, financial education, and food security). While at Georgetown, I was a Board Member of GU Women Coders, Digital Director for The Hilltop Microfinance Initiative, and Social Impact and Outreach Director for the GU Eating Society.

I was featured in a video to launch The Georgetown Initiative on Tech & Society.
