Julianne Zech


Hello world! Welcome to my site.

I'm an incoming software engineer at BlackRock in NYC. I'll be working in the Aladdin Product Group beginning in August 2020.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Georgetown University in 2020 with a double major in computer science and math.

I earned a full tuition scholarship for my senior year from the Clare Boothe Luce Foundation (CBL). I also received a $5,000 summer research grant from CBL and was selected as a Summer Fellow by the Georgetown Undergraduate Research & Opportunities Program, which supported the work that would eventually become my senior thesis. I was one of three undergraduates to receive the Department of Computer Science Excellence Award, awarded to juniors and seniors with the highest achievement.

I'm passionate about technology, product, finance, and sustainability (opportunities for women in STEM, access to capital for small businesses, and food security and nutrition). While at Georgetown, I was a Board Member of GU Women Coders, Digital Director for The Hilltop Microfinance Initiative, Social Impact & Outreach Director for the GU Eating Society. I enjoy adventure travel, trying new restaurants, photography, and watching college basketball.

I participated in a video to launch The Georgetown Initiative on Tech & Society.
