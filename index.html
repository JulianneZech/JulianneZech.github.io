Julianne Zech


Hello world! Welcome to my site.

I'm an incoming software engineer at BlackRock in NYC. I'll be working in the Aladdin Product Group beginning in August 2020.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Georgetown University in 2020 with a double major in computer science and math.

While at Georgetown, I completed a senior thesis titled Identifying Experience Tweets From #MeToo Using Different Machine Learning Models. I began this project during my junior year and worked full-time in Dr. Lisa Singh's Data-Centric Computing Lab during summer 2019. I served as a research scholar at the Massive Data Institute at the McCourt School of Public Policy where I worked with another student on a project to answer questions about the opioid crsis in the United States.

I earned a full tuition scholarship for my senior year from the Clare Boothe Luce Foundation (CBL). I also received a $3,000 summer research grant from CBL and was selected as a Summer Fellow by the Georgetown Undergraduate Research & Opportunities Program (GUROP). GUROP provided an additional $2,000 to support the work that would eventually become my senior thesis. I was one of three undergraduates to receive the Department of Computer Science Academic Excellence Award and was one of three computer science majors awarded funding by the department to attend the 2019 Grace Hopper Celebration.

Some of my professional interests include FinTech, data science, and NLP. While at Georgetown, I was a Board Member of GUWomenCoders, the Digital Director for The Hilltop Microfinance Initiative, an Outreach Director for the GU Eating Society, and completed a study abroad program in Quito, Ecuador. I enjoy traveling, trying new restaurants, and watching college basketball.

I participated in a video to launch The Georgetown Initiative on Tech & Society.
